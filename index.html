Our Mission

The mission of the Metroplex Marines Coordinating Council (MMCC) is to support local military personnel– regular, reserve, and retired; and to support military support charities and volunteer organizations. In short, we don't wait around for some one to take care of things– we take charge (General Order #1) and take care of our own. This is the best place in North Texas to network, find support for our military members, or just share the comraderie of over 7 decades of Marine service.

About Us

Your value of your home starts at the curb.

We normally hold monthly meetings at 1200 noon of the first Thursday of each month with the following exceptions—when the first Thursday falls on 1 January or 4 July. In addition, the November meeting is held on 10 November. All luncheons are held at the TEXO Building (11101 N. Stemmons Freeway Dallas, Texas 75229).

We serve a terrific luncheon ($20 voluntary donation) and learn about what events are going on in the DFW area that we as Marines need to know about.
